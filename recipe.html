Enchanted Bowl of Cereal

Why struggle through a boring bowl of cereal when the Dark Arts are at your fingertips for a little extra fun?

This step-by-step guide will walk you through the necessary rituals, incantations, and inscriptions to make your corn flakes taste like... well, still corn flakes, but with more magic!

  • 591mlMilk of your choice
    Mammalian milk is recommended.

Preparation Items

  • 1No. 2 pencil
    Don't use your No. 1 pencil. Cereal isn't worth that much effort.
  • 6Sheets of letter-size paper
    Increase to 8 if Tuesday.
  • 1Fire extinguisher with at least 50% charge
    This ingredient may be eschewed if the chef/arcane practitioner is a registered eschewer.
  • 1Bowl
  • 1Spoon

Let's Begin!

  1. Place the bowl in the center of a standard ironwood conjuring table.
  2. Arrange the 6 sheets of paper in an inverted Nietzschean hexagram. Stay as quiet as you can.
    Is it Tuesday? Conduct a bi-pole ritual with two additional sheets of paper prior to placing the original 6 sheets. Additionally, instead of staying quiet, state your full name when placing each sheet of paper. Be sure to mind your voice inflection, keeping it even and calm.
  3. Using your No. 2 pencil, inscribe your Chantry progression on each of the 6 sheets.
  4. Perform a Whedon incantation while envisioning your diametric shadow-self.
    Do not envision your analytic shadow-self, as would be usually done.
  5. Take a breath. Clear your mind. Gently, fill the bowl with your cereal of choice.
  6. Add milk.
  7. Extinguish the resulting fire with your fire extinguisher.
  8. Add add parsley in method of your choosing.
  9. Again, extinguish the resulting fire.
  10. Using your spoon, enjoy!
